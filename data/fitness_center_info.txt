Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
How do I sign up for a membership?
You can sign up for a membership online through our website or visit us in person at the front desk.

What are the staffed hours of the gym?
Our staffed hours are Monday to Friday from 6 AM to 9 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8 AM to 6 PM.

Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months in a year. Please contact our support team for more details.

Do you offer guest passes?
Yes, we offer guest passes for $10 per day. Members can bring a guest for free on the first Monday of each month.

For more information, please visit our website or contact us at the details provided above.
